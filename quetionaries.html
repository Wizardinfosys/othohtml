STRAIGHT TEETH WITHOUT BRACES

Orthodontic therapy options available for adults and teenagers that are provided by general dentists and orthodontists are constantly changing. Increasingly, progressive patients and dentists are choosing sequenced removable plastic appliances, such as OrthoSnap orthodontics, instead of traditional metal braces & wires. Now you can get straight teeth without braces.

OrthoSnap New York is on the forefront of the transition to this sophisticated form of orthodontic care. Every year, more and more patients are seeking wireless and bracket-free teeth,.
